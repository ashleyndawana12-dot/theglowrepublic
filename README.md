Project Overview
This project involves the development of a comprehensive website for The Glow Republic, a newly established beauty business based in Pretoria, South Africa. The business was founded by Ashley Ndawana as part of a growing passion for the beauty and skincare industry, with the aim of offering professional and affordable beauty services in a warm and elegant environment.

The website will serve as the digital presence of The Glow Republic, allowing potential clients to learn more about the business, explore available services, and engage with the brand online. As the business is currently in its startup phase, the website plays a key role in establishing brand identity, visibility, and credibility within the beauty industry.

The Glow Republic focuses on enhancing natural beauty through soft, results-driven treatments such as facials, eyelash extensions, and brow services. The digital platform reflects this vision by prioritising elegance, simplicity, and a calming user experience aligned with the brand’s aesthetic.

Mission:
At The Glow Republic, we provide soft, results-driven beauty treatments in a warm and elegant environment, focusing on enhancing natural beauty while creating a relaxing and personalised experience for every client.

Target Audience

The website is designed for women and men aged 20–59 who appreciate gentle beauty treatments and natural enhancements. This includes clients interested in:

Soft, natural-looking beauty results
Skincare and facial treatments
Neat eyelash extensions
Clean and precise brow work
A calming and professional beauty experience
Website Goals and Objectives
Primary Goals

The website has been designed to achieve the following objectives:

Service Awareness:
To clearly present all beauty services offered by The Glow Republic, including descriptions and expected results, so clients can easily understand what is available.

Brand Identity Establishment:
To create a strong and recognisable online presence that reflects the brand’s values of elegance, relaxation, and natural beauty.

Client Engagement:
To provide a simple way for clients to contact the business, make enquiries and book appointments.

Portfolio Showcase:
To display results and treatments through images that demonstrate the quality and style of services offered.

Business Credibility:
To establish trust with potential clients through professional presentation, clear information and consistent branding.

Key Performance Indicators

The success of the website will be measured through:

Number of appointment bookings or enquiries submitted
Website traffic and user engagement
Time spent on service and gallery pages
Contact form submissions
Returning visitors and client retention interest

Key Features and Functionality
Essential Pages Structure

Homepage (index.html):
Acts as the digital introduction to The Glow Republic, featuring a hero section with branding, a short business introduction, navigation menu, featured services, and call-to-action buttons such as “Book Now” or “View Services.”

About Us (about.html):
Provides background information about The Glow Republic, including its founding, vision and mission, brand values, and what sets the business apart in the beauty industry.

Services Page (services.html):
Displays a full breakdown of beauty treatments offered, such as facials, lash extensions, brow treatments, and skincare services. Each service includes descriptions and benefits.

Gallery Page (gallery.html):
Showcases before-and-after images and treatment results to visually communicate the quality and style of services provided.

Contact Page (contact.html):
Includes a contact form for enquiries and bookings, business contact details, operating hours, and location information.

Booking Page (booking.html):
Allows clients to request or schedule appointments by selecting services, preferred dates, and providing personal details.

Core Functionality

Responsive Design:

Contact and Booking System:

Navigation System:
Simple and user-friendly navigation ensures quick access to all pages.

Consistent Branding:
Soft colour palettes, elegant typography, and minimalistic design will reflect the beauty and wellness focus of the brand.

Technical Implementation
Through the use of GitHub and VS Code, the website utilises modern web technologies, including HTML5 for semantic structure, CSS for visual styling and responsive design, and JavaScript for interactive functionality. The responsive framework ensures compatibility across all devices and screen sizes.

Technical Implementation

The website will be developed using standard web technologies including:

HTML5 for structure
CSS3 for styling and layout
JavaScript for interactivity and form handling

The design will prioritise mobile-first responsiveness and smooth user experience, ensuring accessibility across all devices.

Content Strategy

All content will be created to reflect The Glow Republic’s identity as a soft, elegant, and results-driven beauty brand. The tone will be professional yet calming, focusing on confidence, natural beauty, and client comfort. Visual content will be carefully selected to align with the brand aesthetic.

*Testing and Responsiveness*

 *How I tested*

* Opened the website in Google Chrome
* Used Chrome DevTools 
* Tested on different device sizes: iPhone SE, iPhone 12, iPhone 14 Pro. iPad Air, Desktop, Samsung Galaxy A51/71, Nesthub, Nest hub max, Pixel 7

*What I checked*

* Navigation works on all screen sizes
* Text is readable (not too small on mobile)
* Images resize properly
* No horizontal scrolling on mobile
* Gallery scrolls sideways on all devices
* Buttons are easy to tap on phone

*Devices tested (simulated)*

*Breakpoints used in CSS*

* Tablet: 768px - 1024px
* Mobile: 480px - 767px
* Small mobile: less than 480px


*What I fixed*

* Made images responsive with `max-width: 100%`
* Navigation margins adjusted for mobile
* Changed services row from 3 columns to 1 column on phones

📋 Before Your First Visit

Patch testing: Required for all first-time lash lift, tint, and semi-permanent brow clients. This is a quick, painless test to ensure you have no allergic reaction. Please schedule your patch test at least 48 hours before your treatment.

Consultation: Every new client receives a complimentary 5-10 minute consultation before their first treatment. We'll discuss your goals, assess your natural features, and recommend the best approach.

What to bring: Please come with clean skin (no makeup on the treatment area). If you're having lash extensions, avoid wearing mascara or eyeliner to your appointment.
